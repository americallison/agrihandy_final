Steps to sell on AgriHandy

  •   Register a Farmer Account on AgriHandy (Business details, Orange Money number)
  •   Login and post your products on AgriHandy (Include product name, description, price, unit of measure (eg. bag, bucket, etc.), contact number, product location and other related details.)
  •   After a buyer orders your product, you can transport products using AgriHandy delivery service. (Timely and efficient delivery is highly guaranteed with AgriHandy delivery service.)
  •   Receive payment through Orange Money. (Your funds are transferred to your Orange Money account right after the customer receives the products).
Note:
  • Registration is free
  • After a month from registration, a membership fee of $300.00 will be paid per month.
  • AgriHandy will have a 2% commission on every sale made by a farmer.
